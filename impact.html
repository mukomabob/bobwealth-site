Shuvai — "Make a Wish"

Through our school assistance programme, we have helped cover monthly fees for a young girl named Shuvai — whose name means "make a wish." She is completing her Grade 1 in Epworth as of October 2025.

Her journey embodies the hope and transformation we strive to inspire in every life we touch. One child, one family, one community at a time.

Looking Ahead

Our Vision for 2030

Bob Wealth Holdings aims to expand its community impact through measurable, sustainable initiatives that empower Zimbabweans to thrive financially, mentally, and socially.

Target · 2027

Financial Literacy for All

Structured financial literacy and savings education programmes in local communities, beginning with pilot workshops in Epworth and Mabvuku.

Target · 2028

Wellness & Balance

Financial wellness integrated with mental health awareness through community talks and small-group wellness sessions across Zimbabwe.

Target · 2030

Mentorship & Opportunity

A mentorship pipeline linking young professionals and students to financial and career guidance — connecting aspiring talent with industry leaders.
